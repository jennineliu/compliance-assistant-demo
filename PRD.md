Product Name
Border Compliance Assistant

Overview
The Border Compliance Assistant is an interactive travel intelligence application that helps international travelers determine whether items in their luggage may require declaration, documentation, taxation, or are prohibited before arriving at a destination country.
Rather than presenting static customs regulations, the application transforms government guidance into searchable, risk-based decision support.
The initial implementation focuses on Brazil but is designed as a reusable framework for any country.

Problem Statement
International customs regulations are fragmented across multiple government agencies and written primarily as legal or regulatory guidance.
Travelers frequently ask questions like:
	•	Can I bring this?
	•	Will I pay duty?
	•	Should I declare it?
	•	Is this prohibited?
	•	Do I need paperwork?
Finding answers requires navigating numerous government websites and interpreting complex regulations.

Goals
Build a traveler-first interface that
